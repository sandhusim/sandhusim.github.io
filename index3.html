MY FIRST EXPERIENCE IN THE FIELD OF COMPUTER SCIENCE.....

What actually is Computer Science? It is basically the study of computer technology,which includes both hardware and software. Nowadays, a good knowledge and skill about this demanding field surely directs the individual to a better future. Most of the students around the world, are taking this field as a core career options. The institutions and colleges all over the world readily provide the basic knowledge and learning about the field.

I am also pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science branch. When I was in 12th class I was always fascinated by programming and coding.I had a little knowledge about HTML. But, I was always eager to explore the field at the fullest. So, due to my keen eagerness to learn more computer science branch was my foremost choice.

Since, the starting of my semester I have experienced a new and an advanced change in my learning process everyday. With my basic knowledge of HTML i have added up CSS in my learning process. The more I explore a topic, the more I get to know about it. I have also concluded one thing that competition is quite high in this field. Lots and lots of hardwork is required.

I am quite contented and confident about pursuing this branch as a career option. I am sure about the fact that in the coming years, the skills and knowledge I gain will land me at the best place.
